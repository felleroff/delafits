Description:


Line is a container (record) of the form:


<keyword-separator-value-separator-note>.


TFormatLine defines the parameters of parse/compose lines of header, see TLineBuilder.Fmt.


 vaStr: record

   wWidth: ShortInt;

   wWidthQuoteInside: ShortInt;

 end;


Parameters of compose line, String value:

  • wWidth - minimum quantity of characters for text representation;
  • wWidthQuoteInside - minimum quantity of characters for text representation in quotes.


 vaBol: record

   wWidth: ShortInt;

 end;


Parameter of compose line, Boolean value: minimum quantity of characters for text representation.


 vaInt: record

   wWidth: ShortInt;

   wSign: Boolean;

   wFmt: string;

 end;


Parameters of compose line, Integer value:

  • wWidth - minimum quantity of characters for text representation;
  • wSign - to show sign of a number;
  • wFmt - parameter for the function Format.


 vaFloat: record

   wWidth: ShortInt;

   wSign: Boolean;

   wFmt: string;

 end;


Parameters of compose line, Float value:

  • wWidth - minimum quantity of characters for text representation;
  • wSign - to show sign of a number;
  • wFmt - parameter for the function Format.


 vaDateTime: record

   rFmtShortDate: TFmtShortDate;

   wWidth: ShortInt;

   wWidthQuoteInside: ShortInt;

 end;


Parameters of parse/compose line, TDateTime value:

  • rFmtShortDate - short format recording (parse). Default TLineBuilder parses short date format YY/MM/DD;
  • wWidth - minimum quantity of characters for text representation;
  • wWidthQuoteInside - minimum quantity of characters for text representation in quotes.


 vaCoord: record

   rFmtMeasurRa: TCoordMeasur;

   wWidth: ShortInt;

   wWidthQuoteInside: ShortInt;

   wPrecRa: Word;

   wPrecDe: Word;

   wFmtMeasurRa: TCoordMeasur;

   wFmtNotate: TCoordNotate;

 end;


Parameters of parse/compose line, Coordinates (Right ascension α and Declination δ):

  • rFmtMeasurRa - text representation (parse) of Right ascension: degree or hour;
  • wWidth - minimum quantity of characters for text representation;
  • wWidthQuoteInside - minimum quantity of characters for text representation in quotes;
  • wPrecRa - precision of the text representation of Right ascension, number of signs after the decimal point;
  • wPrecDe - precision of the text representation of Declination, number of signs after the decimal point;
  • wFmtMeasurRa - text representation (compose) of Right ascension: degree or hour;
  • wFmtNotate - text representation (compose) of Right ascension and Declination: decimal or hexadecimal.
