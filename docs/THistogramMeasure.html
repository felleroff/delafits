THistogramMeasure

Types ››

Unit: DeLaFitsCommon


Type: type


THistogramMeasure = record

 CountPoints: Int64;

 CountItems: Integer;

 ItemMedian: Integer;

 ItemMax: Integer;

 ItemMaxSmooth: Integer;

 DynamicRangeDefault: THistogramDynamicRange;

end;


Description:


Description of histogram, its characteristics.


  • CountPoints - number of points (pixels of data) which form a histogram;
  • CountItems - number of items (ranks) of histogram;
  • ItemMedian - index of the item of histogram - median;
  • ItemMax - index of the item of histogram - the maximum value;
  • ItemMaxSmooth - index of the histogram - the smoothed maximum of data;
  • DynamicRangeDefault - two indexes of the histogram - the value of the dynamic range by default;
