TCoordNotate

Types ››

Unit: DeLaFitsCommon


Type: type


TCoordNotate = (cmDecimal, cmSexagesimal);


Description:


Text representation of the coordinates (Right ascension α and Declination δ): decimal or hexadecimal.


  • cmDecimal - decimal format: G.(g), e.g: 12.3456 degree or hour (depends on TCoordMeasur);


  • cmSexagesimal - hexadecimal format: GG MM SS.(s), e.g: 12°34'56".7 for Declination, and 12°34'56".7 or 12h34m56.7s for Right ascension (depends on TCoordMeasur);
